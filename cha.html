Charalkunnu Hill Station

A Quiet Retreat Overlooking the Pamba Valley

About Charalkunnu

Charalkunnu is a charming hill station that offers a unique perspective of Pathanamthitta's natural beauty. It is far from the typical tourist trail, making it the perfect spot for those seeking solitude and fresh air.

⛰️ Why Visit?

  • Panoramic Views: Uninterrupted views of the Pamba River and surrounding valleys.
  • Trekking: Gentle trekking trails suitable for all ages.
  • Proximity: Located very close to the cultural hub of Aranmula.
