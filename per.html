Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls

The Great Honey Stream on the Sacred Pamba River

Nature's Grand Cascade

Perunthenaruvi is a popular eco-tourism destination known for its wide, expansive waterfalls rather than its height. The name originates from "Peru" (Great), "Then" (Honey), and "Aruvi" (Stream). The water spreads across a vast area of rugged rocks before plunging 60 to 100 feet into a deep rocky basin.

It is a perfect spot for those who enjoy the raw power of nature and the sound of crashing water. The area around the falls is lush and provides a cooling respite even during summer.
