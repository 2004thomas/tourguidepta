Manneera Waterfalls

A Crystal Clear Forest Cascade near Adavi

About the Falls

Manneera Waterfalls is a serene, multi-tiered cascade located in the Thannithode area of Pathanamthitta. Situated just 3-4 km away from the famous Adavi Eco-Tourism center, it is known as one of the safest waterfalls in the district for families and children.

The water flows through dense forest rocks, creating natural pools that are perfect for a refreshing dip. Unlike larger commercial waterfalls, Manneera offers a raw, peaceful connection with nature.

  • Safety: The lower levels are shallow and safe for kids and women.
  • Three Tiers: Adventurous visitors can climb slightly up to see the higher tiers.
  • Forest Ambience: Located deep within the Konni forest ranges.

üìç How to Reach

By Road: Located on the Konni-Adavi-Manneera road. It is about 15 km from Konni town and 24 km from Pathanamthitta.

Nearby: Can be easily combined with a visit to the Adavi Bowl Boat center.

Entry: Generally open to the public; no formal ticket is required for the falls themselves.

Pro Tip: The rocks can be very slippery, especially during the monsoon (June-August). Wear shoes with good grip and avoid deep areas if you aren't a strong swimmer.
