Pandalam Valiyakoikkal Temple

The Childhood Home of Lord Ayyappa & The Palace Shrine

The Royal Connection

The **Valiyakoikkal Temple** is situated within the Pandalam Palace complex on the banks of the Achankovil River. It is the family temple of the Pandalam Royal House, who adopted Lord Ayyappa (Manikandan) as their son. The temple is modeled after the Sabarimala shrine but is accessible to all devotees throughout the year.

Every year, three days before the Makaravilakku festival, the **Thiruvabharanam Procession** starts from here, carrying the sacred gold ornaments to Sabarimala in a grand trek.

Did you know?

Unlike Sabarimala, women of all ages are allowed to worship at the Valiyakoikkal Temple. It is considered the "Home" of the deity, whereas Sabarimala is his place of "Penance" (Yoga).
