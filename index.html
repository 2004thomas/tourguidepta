Discover Pathanamthitta

Nestled in the heart of Kerala, Pathanamthitta is known for its lush forests, sacred pilgrimage centers, serene waterfalls, and hidden hill viewpoints. From the spiritual aura of Sabarimala to the tranquil beauty of Gavi and Perunthenaruvi, the district offers a perfect blend of nature, heritage, and devotion.

This guide helps travelers explore the most significant and lesser-known destinations across the district, providing essential travel details, nearby facilities, and easy map access — making every journey smoother and more informed.
