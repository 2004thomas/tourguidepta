Nilakkal Mahadeva Temple

The Guardian Deity of the Sabarimala Wilderness

The Gateway Temple

The **Nilakkal Mahadeva Temple** is an ancient shrine situated in the dense forests of the Ranni reserve forest. Lord Shiva is the presiding deity here, worshipped in two forms: a fierce "Ugra" form and a benevolent "Saumya" form. It is believed that Lord Ayyappa himself stopped here to pay respects to his father, Lord Shiva, before his journey to Sannidhanam.

During the Sabarimala pilgrimage season, Nilakkal transforms into the most important transit point where millions of devotees park their vehicles and prepare for the final trek from Pamba.
