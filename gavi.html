Gavi Eco-Tourism

A Pristine Wilderness in the Pathanamthitta District

About Gavi

Gavi is an enchanting eco-tourism destination located in the Pathanamthitta district of Kerala. Part of the **Periyar Tiger Reserve**, it is famous for its untouched scenic beauty, wildlife, and sprawling spice plantations. It gained international fame after being listed by "Alistair International" as one of the leading eco-tourism projects in India.
