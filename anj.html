Anjumala Para

Pathanamthitta's Own "Meesapulimala" experience

The Misty Peak of Enadimangalam

Anjumala Para (Ayiramthooni Mala) is a rising star on the eco-tourism map of Pathanamthitta. Located in Enadimangalam village near Adoor, this hilltop is famous for its "Sea of Clouds" phenomenon during the early morning hours.

The name Ayiramthooni comes from a local legend regarding a miraculous harvest. Today, it serves as a haven for trekkers, photographers, and nature lovers seeking 360-degree views of the Western Ghats.

Location: Kunnida, Enadimangalam (near Adoor).

Distance: 25 km from Pathanamthitta; 12 km from Adoor.

Trek: A moderate 20-30 minute hike from the base parking area to the summit.

Travel Tip: Reach the base by **5:30 AM** if you want to catch the sunrise and the heavy mist. Carry water as there are no shops at the summit.
