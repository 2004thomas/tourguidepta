Parumala St. Peter's & St. Paul's Church

The Sacred Tomb of Saint Gregorios (Parumala Thirumeni)

A Beacon of Faith

The **Parumala Church** is a world-renowned pilgrimage center of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. It is situated on the banks of the Pamba River in the village of Parumala. The church is the final resting place of Saint Gregorios of Parumala, who passed away in 1902 and was canonized in 1947.

The modern church building, designed by the famous architect Laurie Baker, is a circular structure that can accommodate over 2,000 people. It preserves the ancient elements of the original church while embracing a unique architectural vision.
