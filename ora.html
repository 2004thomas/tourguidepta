Orakkanpara Waterfall

The Secret Cascade of Mallappally

A Secluded Retreat

Located near Mallappally in Pathanamthitta, **Orakkanpara** is a mesmerizing waterfall that remains largely untouched by mainstream tourism. It is a favorite among locals for its calm atmosphere and the natural rock formations that create a beautiful multi-tiered water flow during the monsoon season.

Orakkanpara is not a commercialized site. Visitors can enjoy a peaceful trek through rubber plantations to reach the falls. It is perfect for those looking to escape the crowds and enjoy the raw sounds of nature.

The falls are at their majestic best during the Monsoon (June to September) and early winter. During peak summer, the water flow may significantly reduce, revealing unique rock carvings and caves.
