Chuttippara Hill

Panoramic Views & Ramayana Legends in the Town's Heart

The Giant Rock Formation

Rising 200 feet above the landscape, **Chuttippara** is the best vantage point to see Pathanamthitta town. Atop the hill sits the ancient **Harihara Mahadeva Temple**, dedicated to Lord Shiva, which is believed to have been a site where Lord Rama himself offered worship.

  • Kattadipara: A windy hill associated with Lord Hanuman, the son of the wind god Vayu.
  • Pulipara: Features a natural cave (guha) that reportedly served as a shelter for Rama and Sita.

Spiritual Significance

The **Harihara Mahadeva Temple** is a rare sanctuary where the legends of Shiva and Rama intertwine. Every evening, the hill offers a cool breeze and a quiet atmosphere for meditation, despite its proximity to the busy town center.
