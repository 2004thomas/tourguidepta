Sabarimala Sree Dharma Sastha Temple

The Sacred Abode of Lord Ayyappa in the Periyar Wilderness

The Great Pilgrimage

Sabarimala is situated atop a hill (914m above sea level) surrounded by 18 hills in the dense forests of the Periyar Tiger Reserve. Dedicated to Lord Ayyappa, a "Naishtika Brahmachari" (celibate), it is a symbol of communal harmony, welcoming people of all castes and creeds.

The temple is unique as it is only open during specific periods: the Mandala-Makaravilakku season (Nov-Jan), the first five days of every Malayalam month, and during major festivals like Vishu and Onam.
