Chengara Viewpoint

The "Munnar" of Pathanamthitta | Mist, Estates & Sunsets

Experience the Mist

Chengara is an enchanting hill station nestled within the Harrison Malayalam Rubber Estate. It is best known for its "Sea of Clouds" phenomenon where the valleys are completely submerged in white mist during the early morning hours, making it a dream destination for photographers and nature enthusiasts.

  • Sunrise: Watching the sun rise over the rolling hills of the Western Ghats.
  • Estate Roads: Scenic drives through pineapple farms and rubber plantations.
  • Offbeat Vibe: Less crowded than popular hill stations, offering pure tranquility.

üìç Reach & Timings

Location: Malayalapuzha Eram, approx. 12 km from Pathanamthitta town.

Best Time: 5:30 AM to 8:00 AM (for mist) or 5:00 PM for sunset.

Travel: Accessible by car and bike; however, some inner estate roads are better suited for bikes.

Pro Tip: There are very few shops at the viewpoint. It is highly recommended to carry your own snacks and water. Also, combine this visit with the nearby Malayalapuzha Devi Temple (6 km away).
