Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls

Scenic Waterfall Along the River Pamba

About Perunthenaruvi

Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls is a beautiful and serene waterfall located near Vechoochira in Pathanamthitta district. The name literally means "Great Honey Stream." The waterfall flows over rocky terrain along the River Pamba, creating wide cascading streams rather than a single drop. It is an ideal destination for nature lovers, photographers, and families looking for a peaceful outing.

Key Attractions

  • Wide rock formations with flowing water streams
  • Beautiful sunset views
  • Calm picnic atmosphere
  • Forest surroundings and fresh air
  • Photography-friendly landscape

Essential Information

  • Best time to visit: Post-monsoon (September–January)
  • Water level increases during heavy rainfall
  • Entry regulated by local authorities
  • Carry drinking water and essentials

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Ranni (approx. 10 km)

Railway Station: Chengannur (approx. 45 km)

Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (approx. 110 km)

Well-connected by road; parking facilities available near the site.

Walking & Exploration

A short walking trail leads visitors to the waterfall viewpoints. The rocks can be slippery, especially during monsoon season. Visitors are advised to wear proper footwear while exploring.

Entry & Ticket Details

A small entry fee may be collected by local authorities for maintenance. Timings may vary depending on weather and safety conditions. During heavy monsoon, entry may be restricted.

Safety Tips & Warnings

  • Avoid entering deep water zones
  • Rocks can be extremely slippery
  • Do not visit during heavy rainfall alerts
  • Keep children under supervision
  • Avoid littering; maintain ecological cleanliness

Nearby Facilities

  • Small shops and eateries near entrance
  • Petrol pumps in Ranni town
  • Hospitals available in Ranni & Pathanamthitta
  • Basic parking facilities available
