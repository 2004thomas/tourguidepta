Orakkanpara

Hidden Hilltop Viewpoint in Pathanamthitta

About Orakkanpara

Orakkanpara is a scenic rocky hill known for its panoramic sunrise and sunset views. It remains one of the lesser-explored destinations in Pathanamthitta, making it ideal for peaceful trekking and photography.

Key Highlights

  • Panoramic sunrise views
  • Photography hotspot
  • Peaceful green surroundings
  • Short beginner trek

Essential Info

  • No entry fee
  • No booking required
  • Carry drinking water
  • No shops at top

How to Reach

18 km from Pathanamthitta town

Nearest Railway: Chengannur (35 km)

Nearest Airport: Trivandrum (110 km)

Nearby Facilities

Hospital – 18 km

Petrol Pump – 8 km

EV Charging – 18 km

Restaurants – 10 km

Trekking Route

The trek is around 1–1.5 km from the base road. Moderate slope, suitable for beginners. Best visited early morning or evening.
