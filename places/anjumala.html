Anjumala Para

A Scenic Hilltop Escape in Pathanamthitta

About Anjumala Para

Anjumala Para is a scenic hilltop destination in Pathanamthitta district, known for its open rocky terrain and panoramic countryside views. It is a peaceful spot ideal for those looking to escape busy town life and enjoy fresh air and natural beauty.

The name “Anjumala” literally means “Five Hills,” referring to the surrounding hill formations that enhance its scenic charm.

Scenic Highlights

  • Wide-angle valley views
  • Rocky hilltop landscape
  • Sunrise and sunset viewpoints
  • Cool breeze and open sky scenery

Trekking & Exploration

  • Short uphill trek to reach peak
  • Suitable for beginners
  • Rocky surfaces require good footwear
  • Ideal for photography and nature walks

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Pathanamthitta

Railway Station: Chengannur (approx. 30 km)

Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (approx. 120 km)

Accessible by local roads; private vehicle recommended.

Essential Information

  • No entry fee
  • Best time: October to February
  • Visit early morning or evening
  • Limited facilities on hilltop

Nearby Facilities

  • Restaurants and shops in nearby towns
  • Petrol pumps within 5–10 km
  • Hospitals in Pathanamthitta town
  • No permanent stalls at hilltop

Safety Tips

  • Wear comfortable trekking shoes
  • Avoid climbing during heavy rain
  • Be cautious near steep rock edges
  • Carry drinking water
  • Do not litter
