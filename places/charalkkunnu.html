Charalkkunnu Hill Station

A Scenic Viewpoint Overlooking the Pamba Valley

About Charalkkunnu

Charalkkunnu is a picturesque hill station located near Kozhencherry in Pathanamthitta district. Known for its breathtaking views of the Pamba River and surrounding valleys, it is a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

The hilltop offers a calm and refreshing atmosphere, making it ideal for short trips, family outings, and peaceful sunset views.

Scenic Highlights

  • Panoramic view of Pamba River
  • Lush green valleys and hills
  • Beautiful sunrise and sunset views
  • Cool breeze and open viewpoints

Things to Do

  • Nature photography
  • Short walks and light trekking
  • Relaxing picnic with family
  • Bird watching

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Kozhencherry (approx. 5 km)

Railway Station: Chengannur (approx. 25 km)

Airport: Cochin International Airport (approx. 115 km)

Accessible by road; private vehicles recommended.

Essential Information

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • No major entry fee
  • Limited food stalls nearby
  • Evening visits recommended for sunset

Trekking & Trails

Charalkkunnu offers short walking trails and open hilltop areas suitable for light trekking. It is not a difficult trek, but comfortable footwear is recommended. Avoid trekking during heavy rain due to slippery terrain.

Nearby Facilities

  • Hotels and restaurants in Kozhencherry
  • Petrol pumps within 5–10 km
  • Hospitals in Kozhencherry town
  • Small local shops nearby

Safety Tips

  • Avoid visiting during heavy rain
  • Be cautious near cliff edges
  • Carry drinking water
  • Do not litter the hilltop
