Chuttippara

A Quiet Rocky Hill with Panoramic Views

About Chuttippara

Chuttippara is a peaceful rocky hilltop destination in Pathanamthitta district, known for its open landscape and wide countryside views. It is a hidden spot preferred by locals for short evening visits and photography.

The hill offers a calm atmosphere, making it a great place to relax, enjoy cool breezes, and experience scenic sunsets away from crowded tourist centers.

Scenic Highlights

  • Panoramic village and valley views
  • Open rocky hilltop terrain
  • Beautiful sunset scenery
  • Peaceful environment

Things to Do

  • Short uphill trekking
  • Photography
  • Relaxing evening visits
  • Light exploration walks

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Pathanamthitta

Railway Station: Chengannur (approx. 30 km)

Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (approx. 120 km)

Accessible by local roads; private vehicles preferred.

Essential Information

  • No entry fee
  • Best time: October to February
  • Evening visits recommended
  • No permanent shops on hilltop

Trekking & Access

Chuttippara involves a short uphill walk over rocky terrain. The trek is beginner-friendly but requires careful walking. Avoid visiting during heavy rain, as rocks can become slippery.

Nearby Facilities

  • Restaurants in nearby town areas
  • Petrol pumps within 5–10 km
  • Hospitals in Pathanamthitta town
  • No restrooms on hilltop

Safety Tips

  • Wear proper footwear
  • Be cautious near rock edges
  • Carry drinking water
  • Avoid visiting alone at night
  • Do not litter the area
