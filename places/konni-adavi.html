Konni & Adavi

Elephant Camp & Eco Tourism Experience

About Konni & Adavi

Konni is famously known as the "Land of Elephants" and is home to the historic Konni Elephant Training Center. Located amidst forested hills in Pathanamthitta district, Konni offers a mix of wildlife, eco-tourism, and scenic beauty.

Adavi, located nearby, is an eco-tourism destination managed by the forest department, offering bamboo rafting, coracle boating, and nature walks along the Kallar river. Together, Konni and Adavi provide a refreshing nature-based travel experience.

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Konni

Railway Station: Chengannur (approx. 35 km)

Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (approx. 110 km)

Well connected by road; buses and taxis available.

Entry & Booking

  • Entry fee applicable at Elephant Camp
  • Boating tickets available at Adavi counter
  • Peak season may require early arrival
  • Forest department regulations apply

Safety Guidelines

  • Follow forest department instructions strictly
  • Avoid feeding animals without permission
  • Wear life jackets during boating
  • Do not litter; protect wildlife habitat
  • Travel during daytime for better safety

Nearby Facilities

  • Restaurants and shops in Konni town
  • Petrol pumps within 5 km radius
  • Hospitals in Pathanamthitta town
  • Parking facilities available
