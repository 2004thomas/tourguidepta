Mannera Waterfall

A Peaceful Hidden Water Stream

About

Mannera Waterfall is a calm and lesser-known natural stream located in Pathanamthitta district. Surrounded by greenery and rural landscapes, it offers a quiet escape from crowded tourist locations.

Key Attractions

  • Natural rocky stream
  • Ideal for short visits & photography
  • Peaceful countryside surroundings

Essential Info

  • Best time: Post-monsoon
  • Limited facilities nearby
  • Carry drinking water

Safety Tips

  • Rocks may be slippery
  • Avoid heavy rain visits
  • Do not litter
